The Story

Houston Born.
Two Worlds Fused.

PATE comes out of Houston's 072, carrying the South's full weight — the country roads, the concrete, the late nights in the booth. His sound refuses to pick a lane. It's twang-soaked storytelling cut with trap rhythm and Houston swagger, built for people who know that life doesn't fit a single genre.

Repping SWAT Nation, PATE moves independently with a crew-first mentality. Growing Up in the 072 is the statement — real music for real places, real moments, real people.
